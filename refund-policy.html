Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

01Overview

At Nexova, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. This policy explains how and when refunds are issued once our store is live.

02Eligibility

Refunds are considered in the following cases:

  • You received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item
  • Your order was cancelled before it shipped
  • Your returned item has been received and approved by our team

03Refund Timeline

Once a refund is approved, it will be processed within 5–7 business days. The amount will be credited back to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, it may take a few additional days to reflect in your account.

04Non-Refundable Items

Certain items — such as those marked final sale, used or altered products, or items missing original packaging — may not be eligible for a refund.

05How to Request a Refund

Contact our support team with your order details and reason for the refund request. We'll guide you through the next steps.

This policy will take full effect once our store officially launches. For questions right now, reach us at nexovabrand@gmail.com or call +91 98647 05082.
